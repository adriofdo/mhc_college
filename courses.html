Our Courses

Starters

Beginner English for young learners aged 6–8, focused on fun and participation.

Movers & Flyers

Intermediate progression for ages 8–12, building strong listening and speaking skills.

Intermediate & Advanced

Preparation for Cambridge KET, PET and FCE certifications with academic fluency.

Cambridge English Exam Pathway

  1. Beginner

    Basic English for daily needs. Responds to simple directions.
    Option on Completion: STARTERS, MOVERS, FLYERS

  2. Intermediate

    Conversational English with accuracy and fluency.
    Option on Completion: KET & PET

  3. Advanced

    Fluent English usage in academic and social settings.
    Option on Completion: FCE
