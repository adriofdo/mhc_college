About Us

MHC English College, established in 2011, is a privately owned school operated by Mr. Chrishmal Fernando with Mrs. Madhushanika Peiris. Located in Brescia, Vicenza, Padova, and Parma, we offer creative and effective English education for children aged 4 to 18 and adults.

Mr. Chrishmal Fernando

Head Teacher with extensive experience teaching Cambridge English across Europe. Dedicated to student-centered learning and academic success.

Mrs. Madhushanika Peiris

She has a B.A in English, a Diploma in Psychology, English, and Nursing. She began her career in 2013 and has been teaching at MHC English College for 2 years.

Our Vision

To be a caring and collaborative learning community where each student achieves intellectual and personal excellence, and is prepared to promote creativity and excellence.

Our Mission

To ensure every student reaches their full potential through an engaging, inspiring, and challenging learning environment.

Child-Centered Approach

  • Communicates facts and opinions
  • Engages in respectful discussion and debate
  • Understands spoken English at normal speed
  • Holds conversations with native speakers
  • Uses English in real-life contexts like shopping and traveling

Language Development Goals

  • Basic to advanced levels of communication
  • Fluent participation in conversations
  • Understanding idioms and complex grammar
  • Improved academic reading and writing
  • Preparation for KET, PET, and FCE certifications
